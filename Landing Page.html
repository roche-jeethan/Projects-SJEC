About Us

Welcome to GeneHarvest, your trusted destination for all your sperm donation needs. At GeneHarvest, we understand that finding the perfect sperm donor can be an overwhelming journey, which is why we strive to make the process as seamless and stress-free as possible. Our platform is designed to connect prospective parents with a diverse network of highly screened , healthy, and reliable sperm donors. Process for Donation

The process of sperm donation is a meticulous one that requires potential donors to meet certain criteria.

  • 1. They must be between the ages of 18-39
  • 2. Contact us and then visit the center. They need to undergo a series of medical tests to ensure their health and genetic compatibility.
  • 3. Donors must also adhere to the regulations set by the doctor and fulfill any legal requirements of the contract.
  • 4. Once qualified, donors provide a semen sample at a sperm bank, receive payment, and have their sample stored and evaluated before it can be used.
  • 5. Donors are typically not informed of any resulting pregnancies unless specified in the contract.
